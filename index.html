Use arrow keys to move around. If you leave the field, you will wrap arround to the opposite side.

A red circle is blood and means that you are within at least moves of the wumpus.

Green walls are covered in slime and indicates that there is a pit one move away.

Hit Q to begin your attack when you believe you are next to the wumpus. To attack the wumpus use the arrow key in the direction that you believe it is in.
